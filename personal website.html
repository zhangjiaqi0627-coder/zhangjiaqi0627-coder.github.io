About

关于

I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Energy and Power Engineering at Tsinghua University, with a GPA of 4.0/4.0 (Rank 1/12). My research interests span thermo-fluid transport, interfacial and phase-change phenomena, multiscale modeling, and data-driven thermal modeling.

I have hands-on experience in molecular dynamics (MD) simulations using LAMMPS, first-principles calculations with VASP, process modeling in ASPEN Plus, and machine-learning interatomic potential development via the DP-GEN platform. I am also proficient in Python-based data analysis and ML workflows (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn).

I spent a semester as an exchange student at Cornell University (Jan–May 2026), where I took advanced courses in Combustion Processes and Combined Numerical Methods for Fluids. I am passionate about bridging fundamental physics with engineering applications in energy systems.
